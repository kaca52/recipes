Strawberry pie

Description

Strawberry Pie is a delightful dessert that brings together fresh, juicy strawberries and a crisp, golden crust. The natural sweetness of the strawberries is enhanced with a light, sweet glaze, making each bite refreshing and satisfying. Perfect for warm days or special gatherings, this pie offers a delicious balance of texture and flavor, with the perfect combination of fruitiness and a buttery crust.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 6 teaspoons cornstarch
  • 3 ounces strawberry Jell-O gelatin dessert
  • 1-2 pint washed and hulled strawberry
  • 1 9in.baked pastry shell, cooled

Steps

  1. Bring sugar, water and cornstarch to a boil over medium high heat.
  2. Add Jell-O, stir until dissolved
  3. Cool and then add strawberries
  4. Refrigerate about 45 minutes or until it starts to congeal.
  5. Put in pie shell and cool completely
  6. Top with whipped cream or ice cream to serve
