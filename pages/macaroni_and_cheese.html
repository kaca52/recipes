Macaroni and cheese

Description

Macaroni and cheese is a classic, creamy dish loved around the world. The perfect combination of macaroni and rich, melted cheese creates a simple yet delicious meal. It's quick to make and ideal for a satisfying meal when you're craving something comforting and flavorful. Whether prepared as a quick dinner or served as a side, mac and cheese is always a great choice!

Ingredients

  • 16 ounces macaroni
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 dash black pepper
  • 2 cups milk, warmed
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese or 8 ounces cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Cook macaroni according to package directions
  2. In medium saucepan, melt butter over medium heat; stir in flour and cook for 3-5 minutes stirring constantly to form a roux; add salt and pepper; slowly add milk, stirring well after each addition
  3. Cook and stir until bubbly
  4. Stir in cheese a small amount at a time until fully melted
  5. Drain macaroni; add to cheese sauce; sti to coat
